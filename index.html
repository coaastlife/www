    About

    Making medically assisted treatment personal and convenient for the body and mind.

    We offer very private medically assisted treatment (MAT) programs for adults with opioid or alcohol addictions, servicing patients needing to discontinue addictive practices including:

    • those on prescription opioids for pain,
    • senior citizens chronically on opioids, and
    • those needing to return to work or school opioid- or alcohol-free.

    Treatments include Vivitrol, supportive medications for detoxification all complete with psychological and counseling services. Primarily an out-patient service with hospitalization only when needed.
